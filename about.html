About Us

Founded in 2019, we are a group of Youth Change makers who are interested in solving the needs and problems in society and seek to solve them in innovative ways.

We want to discover and connect talented and motivated individuals from all corners of society. Kampong! aims to provide tangible and intangible infrastructure and support to these Youths to solve problems effectively and in a sustainable manner.

We welcome anyone regardless of age or background to join our community of Ground Up initiatives focused on groundwork as we tackle societal issues one unit at a time, filling gaps where larger organisations or groups are unable to.
